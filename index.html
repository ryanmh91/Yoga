About Us

We are here to help you relax and clear your mind. Whether you have just finished a long day at work or are just stressed out, or even if you would just like to try out yoga for the first time we are here for you. We speacialise in restorative yoga, hatha yoga and vinyasa flow yoga. So why not give it a try!

Classes

  • Hatha Yoga

    What Is Hatha Yoga? Hatha yoga focuses on posture and breathing techniques, traditionally to channel vital energy source. In Sanskrit, Hatha translates to force. The practice involves breath, body, and mind, and classes are usually 45 minutes to 90 minutes of breathing, yoga poses, and meditation.

  • Vinyasa Flow

    Vinyasa Flow yoga emphasizes the sequential movement between postures, coordinated with and guided by deliberate breath. The Vinyasa practice becomes a moving meditation that creates strength, freedom and fluidity in the body and mind.

  • Restorative Yoga

    Restorative yoga enables deep relaxation as you holding poses for longer periods of time with the help of props to completely support you. The main focus of Restorative Yoga is that by relaxing in poses, with the aid of props, without strain or pain, we can achieve physical, mental and emotional relaxation.
